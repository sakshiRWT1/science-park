What We Do

What is Science Park?

A Science Park is a cluster of open-air science gadgets designed to make science learning simple, practical, enjoyable, and memorable for school students.

Play-Way Learning

Children naturally learn better while playing. Science Park uses this idea to teach important scientific principles through hands-on activities.

Practical Science

Instead of only reading concepts, students observe, touch, operate, and experience science models in a real environment.

Scientific Temper

These gadgets help students develop curiosity, observation power, logical thinking, questioning ability, and scientific understanding.

Learning Method

Why Schools Need a Science Park

A Science Park inside the school campus creates a permanent outdoor science laboratory. It allows students to connect classroom theory with practical demonstrations.

  • Helps students understand difficult science concepts easily.
  • Encourages group activity, discussion, and experimental learning.
  • Makes the school campus more educational, attractive, and interactive.
  • Suitable for science exhibitions, daily learning, and activity-based education.
