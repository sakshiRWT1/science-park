About Science Park

Transforming school campuses into hubs of scientific discovery

Innovative Learning
Team Based Discovery

Science Park is an innovative concept of teaching science through outdoor educational gadgets. The basic idea is based on children's natural tendency to learn better while playing.

A Science Park inside a school campus allows students to interact with science concepts in a practical manner. These gadgets help develop scientific thinking among students and adults by covering different branches of science.

Our carefully designed installations create an immersive environment where theoretical knowledge transforms into tangible understanding through hands-on experimentation and playful exploration.

Safety First

Safe Usage of Gadgets

Every Science Park gadget is installed with an explanation plate in English. The plate includes comprehensive information to ensure safe and educational use:

  • How to play or operate the gadget safely
  • Scientific principle involved in the gadget
  • Practical application of the concept in real life

The explanation plate is made from etched aluminium in A4 size and can withstand all weather conditions, ensuring long-term readability.
